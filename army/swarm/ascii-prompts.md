# Swarm portraits — 6 paste-able ASCII prompts

For Nano Banana with the SHADOW WOLF / JONINSCII reference image
attached. Same format as the army emblems but **FRONT VIEW** (head-on)
instead of three-quarter — visual signal that swarm members are uniform
and anonymous.

When renders come back, save to `army/swarm/portraits/<name>-ascii-v1.png`.

## Role label table

| Name | Role label | Mask glyph |
|---|---|---|
| SORA | SCOUT | flowing cloud/wind curls |
| SATO | SAGE | two interlocking circles |
| GORO | RUNNER | hammer-and-anvil |
| MINO | WATCHER | single open eye |
| FUDE | SCRIBE | calligraphy brush stroke |
| SOJI | CURATOR | broom with bristles |

## Shared subject template

All 6 prompts share this figure description, with only the mask glyph
varying:

> A hooded uniformed swarm operative, FRONT VIEW (head-on, facing the
> viewer directly, NOT in profile or three-quarter). Plain dark hooded
> robe, shoulders visible at the bottom of the figure. The face is
> hidden under the cowl in shadow EXCEPT for a single distinctive flat
> geometric mask glyph centered on the face: **[GLYPH]**. The figure is
> anonymous and uniform — no individual personality cues, no creature
> features, no distinguishing characteristics other than the mask glyph.
