# System diagram v1 — Tenzo's army + push pipeline

For ChatGPT image-gen. **No reference image** — the painterly Tenzo
portraits would be the wrong medium anchor for a diagram. Describing the
diagram aesthetic from scratch.

3:4 portrait aspect.

```
A clean two-panel system infographic, 3:4 portrait composition, in a
restrained painterly-but-flat illustration style — combining the clarity
of a modern product architecture diagram with the visual identity of an
Asian-fantasy royal court (simplified Avatar: The Last Airbender
production design crossed with a Stripe-style architecture diagram).
NOT a painting. Diagrammatic conventions throughout: clear circular
icons, arrow flows, small typeset labels.

PALETTE (strict): graphite, slate, deep teal, bronze, off-white, thin
gold for connections and labels. NO warm tones. NO orange, NO red, NO
firelight. NO glow effects beyond the thin gold accent lines.

UPPER PANEL — THE DISPATCH COUNCIL (top 60% of the canvas)

Background: a flat soft graphite tone with a faint paper-screen
texture. A single subtle horizontal divider line in dull bronze
separates this panel from the lower panel.

Center, slightly upper: a circular portrait icon of Tenzo (medium-
sized, roughly 15% of canvas width). Inside the circle: a stylized
illustrated profile of a calm Mongolian/Tibetan-read commander, late
30s, dark hair in a low knot, slate-grey high-collared robe, the
bisected-circle insignia visible at the throat. Below the circle, a
label in dull gold serif small caps: "TENZO · COMMANDER".

Behind Tenzo's icon (centered, larger, very faded): the army insignia
— a single circle bisected by one vertical line — rendered large and
faintly in dull bronze as a heraldic watermark on the panel.

Arrayed around Tenzo in a fan / semicircle below and to either side
of his icon, four smaller circular portrait icons (each roughly 10%
of canvas width), each with a clearly distinct silhouette and a label
below. They MUST look completely different from one another:

  [1] LEFT: SENRI — inside the circle: stylized profile of a tall pale
      humanoid alien with elongated smooth cranium and large almond
      eyes, ivory robes. Label: "SENRI · EXPLORE".

  [2] LEFT-CENTER: TAKA — inside the circle: stylized side-profile of
      a hooded raven with a sharp pointed beak and deep cowl. Label:
      "TAKA · OBSERVE".

  [3] RIGHT-CENTER: TETSU — inside the circle: stylized profile of a
      reptilian humanoid in a horned helm with scaled face. Label:
      "TETSU · EXECUTE".

  [4] RIGHT: KAJI — inside the circle: stylized side-profile of a
      figure in a tall horned helm with two large curved horns sweeping
      outward, deep cowl framing a long gaunt undying-style face.
      Label: "KAJI · FORGE".

Connections: thin gold dispatch lines flow from Tenzo's central icon
outward to each of the four subagent icons. Each line is labeled with
a small action verb in dull gold along its length: "READ" (to Senri),
"WATCH" (to Taka), "RUN" (to Tetsu), "RENDER" (to Kaji).

Top-left corner of the upper panel: a small simple human silhouette
icon labeled "ERIK". A thin gold arrow leads from Erik to Tenzo's
icon, captioned "REQUEST" in small gold caps.

LOWER PANEL — THE PUSH PIPELINE (bottom 40% of the canvas)

Background: a flat slightly darker graphite tone, distinct from the
upper panel — a more obviously schematic surface.

A horizontal flow across the panel, left to right:
  [A] A clean folder icon labeled "fixes/context/" on the left
  [B] A right-pointing thin gold arrow
  [C] A wrapped snapshot box (rectangle with rounded corners) with
      the text "NNN-DAY-YYYY-MM-DD" inside, in dull gold
  [D] A right-pointing thin gold arrow
  [E] A simple stylized GitHub icon (branching tree on a small
      server) labeled "GITHUB" below

Below this primary row, a thinner secondary flow showing fresh-agent
onboarding:
  [F] A small simple silhouette labeled "FRESH AGENT"
  [G] A thin gold arrow to a small open-book icon labeled
      "CLAUDE.md + context/README.md"
  [H] A thin gold arrow to a small badge labeled "READY"

OVERALL STYLE:

- Flat illustrated style — NOT a painting, NOT photographic, NOT
  photorealistic. Think high-end product architecture diagram crossed
  with stylized Asian-fantasy iconography.
- Clean confident linework, thin strokes.
- Restrained palette: graphite (background), slate (mid-tones), deep
  teal (accent), dull bronze (frames and the heraldic watermark),
  off-white (highlights), thin gold (connection lines and labels).
- Matte finish, no glossy or wet-look surfaces, no ornate texture.
- Labels in serif small caps, dull gold.
- Generous negative space — the diagram should feel calm and
  navigable, not crowded.
- No decorative elements outside the diagram itself — no moons, no
  stars, no background symbols other than the heraldic watermark
  behind Tenzo.

ASPECT RATIO: 3:4 portrait.
```

## Likely first-iteration corrections (have these ready)

- "The subagents look too similar — make Senri visibly alien with the
  elongated cranium, Taka clearly avian with the prominent beak, Tetsu
  reptilian-scaled not generic-armored, Kaji long-faced and gaunt under
  the horned helm. They should each be unmistakable at icon size."
- "Reduce visual clutter in the lower panel — the pipeline should read
  like a flat schematic, not an illustration."
- "The dispatch lines from Tenzo's icon to the four subagents need to be
  more visible — slightly thicker thin gold."
